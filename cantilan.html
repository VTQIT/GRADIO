About Cantilan

Cantilan is a coastal paradise in Surigao del Sur, known for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant culture. Often called the “Cradle of Towns,” it’s the perfect destination for nature lovers and adventurers alike.

Beautiful Places

Lake Tinago

A serene lake surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for kayaking and picnics.

Cantilan Beaches

Crystal-clear waters and white sandy shores ideal for swimming and relaxation.

Historic Church

A landmark reflecting Cantilan’s rich culture and deep faith.
